### Trends in Zero Trust Security
As of 2024, the global Zero Trust Security market is valued at approximately $40 billion, driven by the increasing need for robust cybersecurity. The market is projected to grow from USD 36.5 billion in 2024 to USD 78.7 billion by 2029 at a compound annual growth rate.

### Implementation Guidelines
The National Security Agency (NSA) is releasing the first two products in a series of Zero Trust Implementation Guidelines (ZIGs) to provide practical, step-by-step guidance on implementing a Zero Trust architecture. The current set of Zero Trust Implementation Guidelines consists of a Primer and three guidelines covering the Discovery, Phase One, and Phase Two stages.

### Federal Mandates
Federal mandates such as Executive Order 14028 and OMB Memorandum M-22-09 require civilian agencies to implement Zero Trust strategies by the end of FY2024.
