# The Rise of AI-Powered Phishing Attacks in 2025
The rise of AI-powered phishing attacks in 2025 is a significant concern, with statistics showing a 1,265% surge in AI-generated phishing attacks since 2023. These attacks are becoming increasingly sophisticated, using AI to craft highly personalized emails that can evade traditional security tools.
## Statistics and Trends
The use of AI in phishing attacks has also led to a significant increase in the number of attacks, with 82.6% of phishing emails detected between September 2024 and February 2025 utilizing AI. This represents a 53.5% year-on-year increase. The average breach cost due to phishing attacks is $4.88M, with BEC losses totaling $2.7B.
## Industries Most Targeted
The industries most targeted by phishing attacks include finance, healthcare, and technology. These industries are particularly vulnerable due to the sensitive nature of the data they handle.
## Combating AI-Powered Phishing Attacks
To combat these threats, experts recommend a multi-faceted approach that combines robust cybersecurity tools, user education, and effective simulation training. This approach can help organizations stay ahead of the threats and protect their sensitive data.
